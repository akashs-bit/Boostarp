Institutional Values & Best Practices


No society or an organization or also an institution like ours can progress unless it is built on values of life and best practices. In view of this our college, management and faculty day in and day out strive to inculcate the values of life like care, concern for needy, love for all, professionalism in working place, work culture in life and many more. Our college has many best practices to its credit especially homework and assignments,annual science exhibition(Chaitanya) have a huge impact on the students mind as they mould their personality in different dimensions of life, be it sparking of creativity, hard work, innovation etc. together they would change the attitude of the students towards betterment.
