Principal Message


Dear Students,
His Holiness Poojya Dr. Sharnbaswappa Appaji, the 8th Mahadasoha Peetadhipati Sharnbasveshwar Samsthan, Kalaburagi; President, Sharanabasaveshwar Vidya Vardhak Sangha, Kalaburagi; Chancellor, Sharnbasva University, Kalaburagi has contributed significantly to the academic learning as the member of Academic Council, Senate and Syndicate of Karnataka University, Dharwad and took over the reins of the Sangha as the Secretary at a young age of 27 years and later as the President of the Sangha in 1983. He has guided the Sangha to blossom into a magnificent educational center of high stature. He blends education with the finest elements of culture by introducing Painting and Music at Graduation and Post Graduation Level without receiving any grants or financial assistance from the Government. Now the Sangha’s graph is always on the rise. His virtues and ideals, wisdom and blessings have laid the path of challenges and milestones in the pursuit of excellence and service. The institutions run by the Sangha have an impeccable track record in academics that bear a testimony to its high stature. The B.A., B.Com, B.Sc, MBA, MCA, BBM, BCA, Engineering and Technology courses have a record of 90% to 100% results.

He started an institution known as Akhil Bharat Anubhava Mantap, which organizes series of lectures and promotes research and other activities. Under the aegis of this organization a fortnightly Journal called “Dasoha Jnana Ratna” is published. The journal is one among the few in India, which exclusively features spiritual, literary and philosophical aspects. Thus he has traversed vast grounds in the propagation of Dasoha and the oneness of man, which is timely in the present context. He has aimed to develop all facets of the society at large. The service of the Sangha under his stewardship is unique in the field of Art, Culture, Language, Literature, Music, Science and Management. He has tried to blend the ancient Indian Spiritualism with a dynamic pragmatism of the modern times and has grafted the western high-technology and modern ideas to the cultural heritage of India. He has always tried to explore new horizons of educational progress and diversification with his vision and dynamism. In thought, words and deeds he has tried to symbolize the spirit of Dasohabhava the supreme form of dedication and selfless service. The educational philosophy of Poojya Dr. Sharnbaswappa Appa describes the spirit of Dasohabhava which implies that the offerings of one’s self and ones service should be in the form of Anjali to God. This spirit which is practiced individually has been institutionalized by him. This enlightened Dasohabhava Sutras composed by him explain his interpretation of concept of Dasoha.

It gives me an immense pleasure in inviting you all to my college and wish to introduce you to its aims, objectives, environment, infrastructure facilities, faculty and the campus. The Sharanabasaveshwar Vidya Vardhak Sangha established in the year 1903 with the aim of imparting quality education was founded by Lingyakya Poojya Doddappa Appa, the 7th Mahadasoha Peethadipati of Sharanabasaveshwar Samsthana. Sharnbasveshwar College of Science was established in the year 1956. All labs are fully equipped and designed as per the needs of the curriculum. The students who seek admission in our college truly feel blessed for its academic ambiance and excellent infrastructure.

Poojya Dr. Sharnbaswappa Appa, the 8th Mahadasoha Peethadhipati emphasizes the need of providing benefits of modern high technology to the students in particular and people in general of this backward region of the state. He continuously ventures in introducing such courses of modern day requirements in the schools, colleges run by the Sangha. The Sharnbasveshwar College of Science has fully developed infrastructure and provides 3 years B.Sc. program leading to Bachelor’s Degree from Gulbarga University, Kalaburagi in different combinations, namely PCM, PME, PMCs, DPM, DPCs, DMCs, DECs & CBZ with intake of 1350 strength of students.

Our college is Re-Accredited (Third Cycle) by NAAC, Banglore, and is affiliated to Gulbarga University, Kalaburagi. I am happy to state that our Institute is adjudged as Grade ‘B’ College by the NAAC, Bangalore. In our Institute, the teaching and non-teaching staff is a blend of senior and experienced as well as young and dynamic faculty devoted to the noble cause of imparting education. In addition to academic activities, the college offers to its students the best sports facilities and a beautiful forum called UMANG for expressing their cultural talents and Science Academy to channelize their academic spirit and genius thus providing them a healthy environment for the overall development of our students.

The institute has a library of area 7200 Sq. Meters that includes open access facility and study zone for students. It has a rich collection of more than 61,247 volumes and many scientific journals and even Magazines for competitive examinations. Our library is having the e-library facility and Inflibnet, Nlist membership, students and staff members can browse 31,35,000 e-books and 6000+ journals with 10 year back volumes. As we are all aware of that “one time is writing is equal to 10 times reading” so we give them home and class assignments. Our college is having spacious class rooms with modern teaching aids like LCD projector and smart boards. The college has English Language Lab with internet facility for access to their needful information and acquisition. We have Hostel facility for Girls and boys separately; we have Students Counseling Cell, Placement Cell, Anti-ragging Cell, Women’s Grievance Cell, Student’s Welfare office, NCC & NSS units for students’ all-round development. We have a vast play ground with different courts. Our college students are supported by many scholarships by Management, Alumni, State and Central Governments. Our College boasts of possessing an excellent, trained and experienced faculty.

Above all the metrics to determine the quality of a college are its results. Since the inception of the college it has been aged hundreds of ranks both at UG and PG level. Our college result of the academic year 2020 – 2021 is very good and has bagged the 6th Rank in B.Sc.

Dr. B. Ramakrishna Reddy
Principal
Sharnbasveshwar College of Science,
Kalaburagi, Karnataka
